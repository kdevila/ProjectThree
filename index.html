About Empow(H)er


Empow(H)er, combining the words Empower and Her, is a festival advocating for girls' secondary education.

Secondary education remains inaccessible across a global scale for young women, due to issues of gender

discrimination and financial poverty. Access to quality education is at risk for so many young women

all over the world. Did you know that currently 120 million girls are out of education/not in school?*


That's why we've partnered up with the Malala Fund.

The Malala Fund is a non-profit that advocates for free, quality education for girls in third-world and developing countries.

They are currently active in the following 10 countries: Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Brazil, Ethiopia, India, Lebanon, Nigeria,

Pakistan, Tanzania and Turkey- with plans to expand their outreach up to 3 countries per year.


In combining our efforts, we can help empower generations of female leaders to come. Ready to join the fight for girls' education?
