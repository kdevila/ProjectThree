About Empow(H)er


Empow(H)er, a combination of the words Empower and Her, is a festival aimed at supporting girls' secondary education.

Through partnering with the Malala Fund, we aim to educate our community on the inaccessibility of such education,

specifically in international countries heavily impacted by issues of gender discrimination and a lack of financial resources.

We hope that through Empow(H)er, opportunities in secondary education amongst young women will increase.

Why the Malala Fund?

Did you know that currently 120 million girls are out of education/not in school?*

The Malala Fund is a non-profit that works in supporting young women in third-world and developing countries,

advocating for policy change, and raising funds for girls' education and networking with local education change-makers.

They are currently active in the following 10 countries: Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Brazil, Ethiopia, India, Lebanon, Nigeria,

Pakistan, Tanzania and Turkey. However, they hope to add up to 3 new countries each year.


In combining our efforts, we can help empower generations of female leaders to come. Ready to join the fight for girls' education?
