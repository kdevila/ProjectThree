Events

Day One: Welcoming Ceremony

Friday, April 25, 2025


10AM-12:30PM

Keynote on girls' education led by co-founder of the Malala Fund, Malala Yousafzai.

Malala will be sharing her inspiring story of overcoming hardship, in pursuit of education as a young woman. She will be giving an exclusive reading of her memoir, "I Am Malala: The Girl Who Stood Up for Education and Was Shot by the Taliban." There will be a meet and greet starting at 11:30AM, and free copies of the book will be given to all attendees.


1PM-3PM

Empow(H)er Sound: Olivia Dean

Enjoy a LIVE performance by English neo soul singer Olivia Dean, as she sings tracks from her first studio album "Messy." Her set will also include covers from other musical artists.


Food and drink will be available all day. Locate the concession stands upon entry.

Day Two: Connecting with Community

Saturday, April 26, 2025


11AM-1:00PM

Empow(H)er STEAM: Enjoy a variety of STEAM-based activities!

Booth 1 ★ Custom vision/manifestation board: Plan your future goals and aspirations.

Booth 2 ★ Crystal geode making: Create your very own crystal geode eggs!

Booth 3 ★ Balloon car racing: Using recycled materials, craft your very own "balloon car."


All required supplies will be available at the event.


1:30PM-3:30PM

Panel Discussion: Malala Fund Spotlight

Join us as we feature a diverse group of speakers, all united in their commitment to empowering and advocating for girls' education.


• Current Malala Fund Fellows will be giving a talk on promoting gender equality in education, and how the fellowship program has transformed their leadership skills.

• Local education advocates and change-makers of the Malala Fund will discuss how they manage the Fund's investments, and how they make change and opportunities happen.


Food and drink will be available all day. Locate the concession stands upon entry.

Day Three: Empow(H)er Finale

Sunday, April 27, 2025


10AM-12:00PM

Empow(H)er Goals

To commemorate Empow(H)er's final day, two writing "wrap-ups" will be offered.


• Commitment Wall: Attendees will write on sticky notes and share their personal pledge of commitment to advocacy/promoting change in girls' secondary education.

• Letters of Impact: Write letters/words of affirmation to be passed out to girls within the Malala Fund programs. Help create inspirational keepsakes for their educational journey!


12PM-8PM

Emigran Tulip Festival

Spring has arrived at Emigran Park! In honor of the Istanbul Tulip Festival, Emigran Park will be unveiling several installations of tulip gardens. Experience the beauty of nature and the vibrant colors of the florals. This is a free event, open to all.


Food and drink will be available all day. Locate the concession stands upon entry.
