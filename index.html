About Empow(H)er


Empow(H)er, a combination of the words Empower and Her, is a festival aimed at supporting girls' secondary education.

Did you know that currently 120 million girls are out of education/not in school?*

Through partnering with the Malala Fund, we aim to educate our community on the inaccessibility of such education specifically in international countries

heavily impacted by issues of gender discrimination and a lack of financial resources. The Malala Fund works in supporting young women in third-world

and developing countries, advocating for policy change, and raising funds for girls' education.


In combining our efforts, we can help empower generations of female leaders to come. Ready to join our efforts?
